Authentication

To use the program, you must authenticate yourself. To login, use your username and password. Note: your username is the same as your email address used when signing up.

Enter your username and password into the appropriate fields.

If you are a manager / supplier, you will be prompted for your OTP code, which can be found in your 2FA app of your choosing.

 

Registration

To register, click on the button in the authentication screen, and you will be brought here:

Enter the details as requested, and then proceed. If you are registering as a supplier, you will be guided on how to setup your 2-factor authentication (2FA).

 

 

Home Screen

This screen serves as a gateway to all the other main functions of the program. You can use the menu bar on the left (hover over the menu icon) to access other forms.

You can get a quick overview of how the business is performing on this screen.

Note: The statistics shown differ depending on the user type.

 

 

New Order

This is where a new order is created for customers.

Customers are selected using the combo box, and then the order is assigned to them. You can add items, remove items, and once done, click save order. You can enter the amount paid to view the balance left of the order.

You have the option to also export the order, where it gets saved as a text file, and also optionally emailed directly to the customer.

 

 

Manage Customers

Here, customers can be managed, and their orders can be viewed / deleted.


 

Add a Customer

Customers can be added simply through this form.

 

 

Inventory Management

This form is only available to suppliers and managers

Here, suppliers and managers can view products, and edit / remove them. Managers can also request stock, which will directly email the supplier with a request for a restock. The grid can also be filtered to meet the needs of the user.

 

To edit a product, click on edit product, change its details, and then click the button again to commit the changes.
